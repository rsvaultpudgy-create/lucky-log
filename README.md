# Lucky Log

A RuneLite side panel for chasing drops: every boss's full loot table, a dry-streak calculator, a live loot feed, and your all-time totals — all in one place.

## Features

- **Full drop tables** for 70+ bosses, all three raids (CoX / ToB / ToA, normal *and* hard/challenge mode), every clue tier, Barrows, both Gauntlets, Moons of Peril and more — notable uniques *and* common drops, with exact `1/x` rates pulled from the OSRS Wiki.
- **Dry-streak calculator** — pick a goal item and see how many kills you've gone dry plus the "% of players who'd have it by now," with the line fading white → green as the streak stretches.
- **Per-kill loot feed** — every kill shows its items with icons and (optionally) the rarest drop's odds.
- **Total Loot** — all-time totals per boss with live Grand Exchange value, colour-coded white → green → blue → purple as it climbs.
- **KC / pull history** — the exact count you hit each unique on.
- **Smart raid dry-calc** — Chambers of Xeric, Tombs of Amascut and Theatre of Blood factor in your points (and deaths) instead of a flat rate.
- **Per-reward-pull tracking** — Wintertodt, Tempoross and Guardians of the Rift count each reward you pull from the cart / pool / guardian rather than per game.
- **Search + auto-jump** — type to filter the boss list, and the panel automatically switches to whatever boss you just killed.

## Usage

1. Open the **Lucky Log** panel (star icon in the sidebar).
2. Search or pick a boss, and optionally a **goal item**.
3. Use **Set KC** / **Set pulls** to enter your existing count so the maths is right from day one.
4. Toggle **Total Loot** to switch between the recent-kill feed and your all-time totals.

## Notes

- Drop rates are sourced from the [OSRS Wiki](https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/) and converted to exact per-kill probabilities.
- Uses only loot events and local RuneLite config storage — no external files, no network calls.
